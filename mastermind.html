How to Play

Objective
Crack the secret 4-color code in 10 guesses.
Making a Guess
  • Tap a peg slot in the active row to select it.
  • Tap a color in the palette to fill it. The selection auto-advances to the next empty slot.
  • Tap any filled slot to re-select it and change its color.
  • Once all 4 slots are filled, tap Check Guess.
Feedback Pegs
Gold — right color, right position
Silver — right color, wrong position
Empty — color not in the code
Colors can repeat. Feedback pegs don't indicate which slot they refer to.
