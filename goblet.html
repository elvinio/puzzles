Goblet Gobblers

📖 The Legend of the Goblets

The World

Two clans — the Gold and the Blue — battle for dominance on a 3×3 arena. Each clan fields six goblets: two Large, two Medium, two Small. Get three of your colour in a row to claim victory.

The Gobbling Hierarchy

Goblets are hungry creatures. A Large goblet can swallow a Medium or Small. A Medium can swallow a Small. A Small is humble — it can only settle on empty ground. You may gobble your own kin or your enemy's — no loyalty in this arena.

The Opening Ritual

The first two moves of the game are sacred. Each clan must send forth a Small goblet to begin. No Large, no Medium — only the meek shall open the battle.

The Ungobble

A goblet that sits atop a stack may be picked up and redeployed — but it cannot return to the very spot it left. Beware: lifting a goblet may reveal a hidden foe beneath, and if that reveal completes a row of three… your opponent wins before you even place.

Victory

Three goblets of the same colour — horizontal, vertical, or diagonal — end the battle. The winning cells will pulse with glory. Press Play Again to start a new war.
