🚦 Car Jam — How to Play

Objective

Deliver enough people each level before the queue overflows or someone's patience runs out.

The Queue

  • People of different colors line up — max 10 slots.
  • If a new person arrives and all slots are full → Game Over.
  • Each person has a patience bar. If it hits zero → Game Over.
  • You can't reorder the queue — work with who's at the front.

Vehicles

  • 🚗 Car — 2 seats, fast road trip & fast cooldown.
  • 🚐 Van — 4 seats, medium speed.
  • 🚌 Bus — 6 seats, slow but high capacity.
  • Each vehicle only picks up people of its own color.
  • After returning, it needs to cool down before dispatching again.

Road & Jam Meter

  • 3 lanes available. All lanes full = can't dispatch until one returns.
  • When all 3 lanes are in use, the Jam Meter fills. If it maxes out, all vehicles freeze briefly while the queue keeps growing.

Special People

  • ⭐ VIP — Must board first. If other same-color people board ahead of them, the entire load scores nothing.
  • 🩶 Blocker — Grey, no vehicle matches. Tap them to dismiss (takes 2 sec).

Scoring

  • +10 per person delivered.
  • +50 full load bonus (filled to exact capacity).
  • +30 combo for 3 correct dispatches in a row.
  • −20 wasted run (no matching color in queue).
  • Patience multiplier: 🟢 >66% = ×2 · 🟡 33–66% = ×1 · 🔴 <33% = ×0.5
