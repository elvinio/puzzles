2048 — How to Play

Basics

Swipe or use arrow keys to slide all tiles. When two tiles that can merge collide, they combine into one. A new tile spawns after each move. Reach the goal tile to win!

Classic

Tiles are powers of 2. Equal tiles merge and double (2+2=4, 4+4=8…). Goal: 2048

Fibonacci

Tiles follow the Fibonacci sequence (1, 2, 3, 5, 8…). Consecutive neighbours merge to the next value: 1+2=3, 2+3=5, 3+5=8… Equal tiles do not merge. Goal: 2584

Threes

Spawns 1s and 2s. Special rule: 1+2=3. After that, equal tiles merge (3+3=6, 6+6=12…). Goal: 3072

Power of 3

Tiles are powers of 3. Equal tiles merge and triple (3+3=9, 9+9=27…). Goal: 2187

Alphabet

Tiles are letters A–Z. Equal letters merge to the next (A+A=B, B+B=C…). Goal: K
