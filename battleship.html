How to Play
1️⃣
Place ships — tap a ship in the tray, then tap the grid. Press Rotate to change orientation.
2️⃣
Tap enemy waters to fire. Hit = red dot, Miss = grey dot.
3️⃣
Sink all 5 enemy ships to win. The computer fires back after every turn.
♻️
Re-place ships at any time during placement by tapping a placed ship in the tray.
Classic
Take turns firing one shot. First to sink all 5 ships wins.
⏱ Blitz
8-second countdown per shot. If time runs out your turn is forfeited. Computer always fires instantly.
🔱 Salvo
Fire one shot per surviving ship per turn (starts at 5). As ships sink, you fire fewer shots. The computer does the same.
⚡ Power-Ups
Every confirmed hit earns a random power-up stored in your inventory (max 3 each):
🔍 Radar — reveals a 3×3 area without firing
💥 Cluster — fires at a 2×2 area
🚀 Torpedo — fires the entire selected row or column (all 15 cells)
